According to this website all you need to make guacamole is avocado and salt, this is false and this advice should be completly ignored. To make good guac you need lime juice, some pepper, and cilantro.

Ingredients

  1. 2 ripe avocados
  2. lime juice
  3. salt, doesn't have to be kosher
  4. serrano chiles - can be substitued by red pepper flakes or green peppers.
  5. cilantro
  6. black peppers
  7. tomato, chopped very finely
  8. garlic powder
